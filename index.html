エッセイ・テンプレート（英検1級）

データ（数値・研究）と実例で論証する2理由型。色付き部分を差し替えるだけ。「模範解答例」の全10本はこの型で作成しています。

Introduction
The importance of A has grown increasingly evident in recent years, sparking considerable debate. While some argue that B suffices, I believe A is ultimately more crucial. My reasoning is based on two key points.

Body 1｜Pattern A（データ）
Firstly, A is indispensable because it directly addresses 課題. A recent study demonstrates that A achieves 数値 compared to 比較対象, with measurable outcomes in 文脈. This comparative advantage renders A fundamentally superior for 応用, thereby underscoring why B's sufficiency is untenable.

Body 2｜Pattern B（実例）
Secondly, A has proven effective in real-world implementation. 具体例（国・地域・企業） exemplifies this—the results have been 実績. Importantly, similar outcomes have been documented elsewhere, suggesting that A's benefits aren't context-dependent. This demonstrates that A works not just theoretically, but practically.

Conclusion
In conclusion, the evidence clearly shows that A is essential to 領域. While some challenges remain, they can be addressed through continued innovation and strategic effort. It is therefore imperative that stakeholders prioritize A and commit to its further advancement.

💡 数値・研究機関・具体例が説得力の核。各「模範解答例」のテンプレ対応表で当てはめ方を確認できます。

テンプレ・ビルダー

型を選び、空欄を埋めると原稿と語数を自動生成します。理由は2つでOK（3つ目は任意）。

    模範解答例

    公式サンプル全10問の模範解答。各例は新テンプレート（データ＋実例の2理由型）で作成。タイトルをタップで解答が開き、テンプレ対応表で当てはめ方を、Q&A（各4問）で展開を確認できます。

    Q&A攻略のコツ（4分・5問程度）
    ① 質問の意図を一言で受ける（That's a good point. / Yes, I think so.）→ ② 理由を1つ→ ③ 具体例や言い換えで補強、を 3〜4文で。
    反論・弱点を突かれても、勝ち負けではなく語学の試験。That's a good point, but… と受けてから自分の立場を述べればOK。詰まったら Well, … / Let me see. で間をつなぐ。
