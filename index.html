エッセイ・テンプレート（英検1級 standard）

洗練版。データ（数値・研究）と実例で論証する2理由型。色付き部分を差し替えるだけ。「模範解答例」の全10本はこの型で作成しています。

Introduction
The importance of A has become increasingly evident in recent years, giving rise to considerable debate. While some argue that B is sufficient, I believe A is fundamentally essential. This view is supported by two key considerations.

Body 1｜Pattern A（データ）
First, A is indispensable because it effectively addresses 課題 in ways that B cannot. Recent research demonstrates this clearly: A shows measurable advantages, achieving 数値 compared to 比較対象, with significant implications for 文脈. This empirical evidence indicates that A is substantially more effective than B for 応用.

Body 2｜Pattern B（実例）
Second, A has proven effective in real-world contexts. 具体例（国・地域・企業） provides a compelling example—the results have been 実績 in scale. Moreover, similar successes have been documented in other regions and sectors, suggesting that A's benefits are not limited to specific contexts. This demonstrates A's practical value and broad applicability.

Conclusion
In conclusion, the evidence strongly supports the position that A is essential to 領域. Though certain challenges remain, they can be addressed through ongoing innovation and strategic planning. It is therefore crucial that stakeholders commit to advancing A further.

💡 各「模範解答例」のテンプレ対応表で当てはめ方を確認できます。数値・研究・具体例が説得力の核。

テンプレ・ビルダー

型を選び、空欄を埋めると原稿と語数を自動生成します。理由は2つでOK（3つ目は任意）。

模範解答例

過去5回分（Aコース・各5問）＋初期テンプレ版を収録。上のサブタブで回を切替。各例はテンプレで作成し、タイトルをタップで解答・テンプレ対応表・Q&Aが開きます。

Q&A攻略のコツ（4分・5問程度）
① 質問の意図を一言で受ける（That's a good point. / Yes, I think so.）→ ② 理由を1つ→ ③ 具体例や言い換えで補強、を 3〜4文で。
反論・弱点を突かれても、勝ち負けではなく語学の試験。That's a good point, but… と受けてから自分の立場を述べればOK。詰まったら Well, … / Let me see. で間をつなぐ。
